what we collect:
- username, email, password (hashed)
- your slates (encrypted)
- visit count, storage used
- stripe data (if you donate)
- ip address (for security)

how we use it:
- authenticate you
- save your slates (encrypted to backblaze b2)
- send verification emails (via resend)
- process donations (via stripe)
- prevent abuse

zero-knowledge encryption:
- your private slates are encrypted with your password
- we CANNOT read them
- lost password = lost data forever
- published slates are public (not encrypted)

who sees your data:
- stripe (payments)
- backblaze (encrypted storage)
- resend (emails)
- nobody else. we don't sell data or use ads.

your rights:
- export your slates
- delete your account
- hide supporter badge
- manage stripe subscription

no tracking:
- no cookies, no analytics, no third-party tracking
- we use localStorage for your login token

security:
- https only
- passwords hashed with bcrypt
- content encrypted with aes-256-gcm
- rate limiting

data deletion:
- delete your account anytime
- all data gone within 24 hours

questions? email [your email] or visit justtype.io
